Content Guidelines

Vilebin provides a platform for users to upload text information without fear of censorship. Vilebin reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to remove any content from the platform for any reason, including but not limited to violations of this policy.
The following content is prohibited on our platform:
  • Personal information of individuals under the age of 15
  • Links to explicit material involving minors
  • Dox requests or any form of targeted harassment
  • Spam or malicious content
  • Direct threats of physical harm, terrorism, or swatting
  • Reposting identical or low quality content
